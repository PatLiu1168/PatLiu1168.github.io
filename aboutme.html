In the past, I've worked at Blackberry QNX as a member of the Core OS Test Team and the Autonomous Vehicle Innovation Centre (AVIC). With the Core OS Test Team, I was responsible for writing regression tests that verify the thread-safe and signal handler safe capabilities of libc API in the QNX RTOS. This experience was a fantastic opportunity to learn about multithreading, microkernel operating systems, and message passing. With AVIC, I was involved with the porting of ROS2 and AutowareAuto to QNX. I also worked on integrating the drive-by-wire system of a Lincoln MKZ to AutowareAuto and created a ROS2 interface for kvaser devices. Not only did I gain a deeper understanding of QNX, but I've also become more comfortable with cross-compiling, CMake, and the canbus.
